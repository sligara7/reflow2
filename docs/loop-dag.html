Reflow 2.0 · process model

The coherence loop, as a DAG

Six phases, the feedback edges that keep them honest, and the axis that makes those edges safe to follow. Coloured by what reflow2 can actually do about each one today.

The forward spine is the easy half and it works. The interesting edges point backwards: a failing test forces a fix, and a fix that changes behaviour has to travel back up into the function and the allocation, or the design quietly becomes fiction. Axis Z is what makes travelling backwards safe — the prior state is snapshotted at an epoch, so the design can follow the build without losing what was first intended.

Reading the backward edges

The two amber edges are the ones that decide whether a release matches its design. They are reachable today — recording the fix as a change at an epoch and updating the capability works, and the original wording survives in a snapshot. What is missing is that nothing asks. A run that does it and a run that does not both end with reflow2 reporting the design coherent.

Five fix cycles, one of which widened an idempotency window from 24 hours to 7 days without touching the design. At release, detect_gaps returned [].

What reflow2 can hold of this model

Modelling the diagram above inside reflow2 is the sharper test — its own claim is “design anything,” and its operating model is a design. Four things got in the way.

FrictionWhat happened
no write side Flow is fully specified — flow_type: process, entry and exit points, PART_OF_FLOW from Capability — and has no constructor and no tool. The one type meant for “an ordered process linking capabilities end to end” cannot be created. This model is its exact use case.
semantics lost Forward “feeds” and backward “forces a resync” both had to become TRIGGERS, which is declared * → * and carries no role. The backward edges are the entire subject and the graph cannot tell them from the forward ones.
cycles invisible The loops are the point, and circular_dependency does not fire — it walks DEPENDS_ON and contracts, not TRIGGERS. A process model's cycles are its most important feature and nothing reads them.
product-shaped concept_without_design fired: zero Components. A process has no components, so the phase detectors assume the subject is a product. Execution evidence for the open question of designing non-software things.
