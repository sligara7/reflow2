# Trial records

Findings from real use of reflow2, recorded verbatim (see COORD.md's conventions).
The trials here exercise **reflow2 on itself** — blind trials, self-host runs, and
the erosion/coherence/phase-coverage instruments — and are the public evidence base
[the backlog](../backlog.md) cites.

Trials that point reflow2 at **other real systems** (brownfield adoptions, personal
projects) are kept out of the public repo, in a local `docs/trials-private/`
directory, because they describe designs that are not reflow2's to publish. The
backlog summarizes their findings where relevant; the raw records are private.
